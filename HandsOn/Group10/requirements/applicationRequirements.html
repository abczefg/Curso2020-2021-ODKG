Application: ------------ The Budgetary Execution of the 2019 Municipal Budgets of the Madrid town hall is an information that is published in a very detailed way and it is free to read to anyone who wants to do it. However, due to the complex this information is, it could be difficult to interpret and, usually, it do not allow to look for some specific information. The application we are going to built will summarize this information and will show it in a user-friendly interface. This application will allow users to look for specific information, to clasify it by categories and to obtain some statistic and graphs.
