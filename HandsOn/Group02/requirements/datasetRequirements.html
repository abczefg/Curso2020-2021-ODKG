Description: Basic data of the contracts awarded in 2020 by the different Public Administrations and other public sector entities subject to the aforementioned regulations. It includes, among other data, the contracting entity, the type of contract, the contract and advertisement data, and the link to the documentation. Also, a dataset about about City District Boards that act as the contracticting bodies is included. Relevant information abaut tose entities (such as contact information, address...) can be found in this dataset.

Data is divided in two datasets:

  • Contractual activity 2020- Madrid City Council
  • General data about City District Boards

R1. Pertainance to the domain

All datasets selected belong to the public procurement domain domain as they provide information about the public contracts in the city of Madrid. This information is uptdated monthly.

R2. Format

All datasets selected are available in CSV format.
