Dataset Requirements

  • R1: The datasets is related to the public procurement domain. In particular, the data is related to contracts and purchases over $5000 of the Californian State during the years between 2012-2015.
  • R2: Data is available in csv.
  • R3: The dataset has an open license.
  • R4: The data contains company names and locations (ZIP code, coordinates) that can be linked with generic real-worl entities.
  • R5: There is documentation available for the dataset ("purchase-order-data-metadata.csv").
