Dataset Requirements

Dataset ContratosEmergenciaSMS.csv

R1: The dataset contains information regarding the contracts carried out on an emergency basis during the recent Covid-19 pandemic by the Murcia Health Administration (SMS), satisfying the domain requirement.

R2: The dataset is a .csv file.

R3: The dataset has the license specified in https://datosabiertos.regiondemurcia.es/avisolegal where it is detailed that these general conditions allow the reuse of the documents submitted to them for commercial and non-commercial purposes. Authorized reuse includes, by way of illustration, the activities of copying, disseminating, modifying, adapting, extracting, rearranging and combining information. This authorization also entails the free and non-exclusive transfer of intellectual property rights, where appropriate, corresponding to such documents, authorizing the reuse of activities of reproduction, distribution, public communication or transformation, necessary to develop the authorized reuse activities. , in any modality and under any format, for everyone and for the maximum period allowed by law.

R4: By having fields such as awardee or product type, it is possible to establish relationships with other entities in other different datasets.

R5:The dataset only has a brief description, not detailed documentation of it.

R6: In this case, only the Region of Murcia appears as publisher.

R4: This dataset contains a variable called OrganisationCode, which is a Linked Data Resource, OrganisationName, which is a location, and Language, which contains the language/s the translation/interpretating was done.

R5: Additional documentation is linked on the webpage, but there is no documentation at all, just a brief summary of the content.

R6: The data comes from an only data source: the Blaby District Council.

Dataset Electricity.csv

R1: This dataset contains the consumption of electricty on schools at Leeds City and the price it was paid for it.

R2: The dataset is on a csv format.

R3: The dataset has an open license, where you can copy, publish, distribute and transmit the Information, adapt the Information, exploit the Information commercially and non-commercially for example, by combining it with other Information, or by including it in your own product or application. But also you must acknowledge the source of the Information in your product or application by including or linking to any attribution statement specified by the Information Provider(s) and, where possible, provide a link to this licence.

R4: The dataset contains locations which can be easily linked with generic real world entities.

R5: More information is available at the web page of Leeds City Council, but no very detailed.

R6: The data sources are two: The Leeds City Council and Engie UK (energy contract supplier).
