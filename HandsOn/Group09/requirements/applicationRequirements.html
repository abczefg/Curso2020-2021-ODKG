Application Requirements

Application Emergency Contracts Covid-19

The application will be able to show the information regarding the budget allocated, in this case, by the Murcian health administration, for the creation of emergency contracts to combat the Covid-19 pandemic. Differentiating between the type of products and the quantity available and pending receipt.

Application Energy Consumption

The application could perfom an energy consumption tracking and provide some alerts if any error or strange date is generated on the dataset.

Application translation costs

The application would allow searching between the different payments made by the Blaby District Council for the services of transcription and/or translation, showing the date of the payment, department, payee, language and amount. The user would also be allowed to filter by the different types of variables.

Application minor contracts

The application would allow to search by the different dates, recognition number, contract object, contractor organization, NIF and deadline. The user will be able to filter by price as well
